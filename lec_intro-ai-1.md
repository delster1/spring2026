# Intro to AI  - Lec1

**AI vs Machine Learning**
- AI - AI
- ML - techniques applying AI using statistical methods that allow AI to improve with experience

**History**
- 1988 - First Convolutional NeuralNet
    - "Gradient Based Learning Applied to Document Recognition" - Yann LeCun, Leon Bottou, Yoshua Bengio, Patrick Hafner
- 1997 - Deep Blue beats Kasparov
- 1997 - Sequence Processing
    - "Long short-term memory" - Sepp Hochreiter, Jurgen Schmidhuber
- 2012 - first Deep Learning, accomplished task
    - "ImageNet Classificaiton with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks" - Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, Geoffrey E. Hinton
- 2020 - GPT-3 
- 2024 - ChatGPT & GPT4
- Agentic AI & Multimodal
