 ### Summary of presentation (150-500 words)
Trent talked about his time in industry, from Amazon, a startup called Applican, some business ventures, and more. He's managed many projects and done a lot of work throughout his career. At a startup, he learned a lot about how to get involved with a project and the business side of the tech industry, which inspired his interest in business that he'd explore later in his career. In mentioning his time at Google, he mentioned the importance of having a good manager and getting them to be willing towork with you. Having a bag manager can have an immensely negative impact on one's career, and leveraging a good one can do the opposite. At google, he worked on an ads measurement team, identifying whether ads worked and measuring changes in a products sucess based on google's ads. His next job was in options market trading, where he relied heavily on his experience in math and statistics, saying his engineering background made him much more successful here. As a quant trader, he mostly wrote code and developed Machine Learning models, worked with researchers on identifying tasks, and actively bought / sold options. He said this was an incredibly fast-paced and stressful environment, but it was incredibly valueable.
