## IB - Def & Basics

**globalization** 
- shift toward an interdependent and integrated global economy
- for markets:
    - trade barriers falling and buyer preference change
- for production:
    - a company can now source goods & services easily from other countries

**international business** 
- all cross-national exchange of goods, services, rescources between multiple nations 
    - exchange of money, goods, resources, intellectual property, contractual assets, etc.

- entities involved may vary
    - large multinational companies
    - small one-person firms

### vocab!
*business* - person or organization engaged in commerce 
*importer* - organization selling products sourced from other countries
*exporter* - organization selling products and services sourced from it's home country
*foreign direct investment* - investment of foreign assets into domestic structures, equipments, organizations
*location advantages* - advantages due to choice of foreign mkt 
    - raw materials, cheap labor, key suppliers, customers, natural resources, energy, etc.
