# Musc 296 - Jazz Intro
By the end of this online course you should be able to...
- Identify the major styles of jazz and the roots influences of jazz
- Demonstrate an understanding of the basic characteristics of music
- Describe the characteristics styles of jazz with appropriate musical terminology
- Demonstrate active listening skills
- Analyze how jazz has influenced American music, culture, diversity, and society

Course Schedule
This class is designed for you to work at your own pace within the periods delineated by the 3 exams on:
- March 2-3(chapters 1-4 and 6)
- April 13-14 (chapters 5 and 7-11),
- May 14-15 (chapters 12-15).

Want more structure? 
- You can follow the suggested syllabus on the last page.

## Course Basics / Structure

**blogs** 
- 10 total blog posts
- on topics in units 3-16
- no AI 
- 250 words

**soft chalks - "lectures"**   
- web lessons w/ pictures, diagrams, audo stuff, etc.
- spend 10 mins for full credit 

**discussion forums** 
- 64 total comments for 100 points
- participitation opportunity questions, student replies, etc.

**chapter & listening quizzws** 
- sharpen listening skills & vocab
- test comprehension
- unlimited attempts

**exams** 
- open at 6 am on first day and close at 11 pm second day
