**Project** 
- Week 1: Teams formed

developed
- Week 3: Initial architecture & 1 st sprint requirements list developed
- Week 4: 1st sprint delivered & 2nd sprint requirements list developed
- Weeks 6, 8, 10, and 12: sprints 2-5 delivered & requirements list for sprints 3-5 and
final sprint developed
- Week 14: Final sprint delivered
